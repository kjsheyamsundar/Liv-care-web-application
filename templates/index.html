Liver Disease Prediction

Liver diseases encompass a wide range of conditions that affect the liver's structure and function, including hepatitis, cirrhosis, fatty liver disease, and liver cancer. Early detection and timely management are crucial for improving patient outcomes and reducing the burden on healthcare systems. Machine learning and data analysis techniques are often utilized to develop predictive models for liver disease. These models are trained on large datasets containing information from individuals with and without liver diseases. By analyzing patterns and correlations within the data, the models learn to identify risk factors and predict the likelihood of liver disease occurrence in a given individual.
